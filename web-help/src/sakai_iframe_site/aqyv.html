Home/Worksite Information: Overview

What it does

On the Home page of each course site or project site, you will see areas for general information (e.g., Worksite Information) and synoptic views for communication tools (e.g., Recent Announcements, Messages and Forums Notifications, and Recent Chat Messages) used in your site. Additionally, you can view and alter your privacy status for the site.

Key concepts

Content options: You can display a webpage or text in your Worksite Information box. For instructions, see Adding content to the Worksite Information box.

Privacy status: You can choose to hide your identity, thereby preventing other participants from contacting you via the site. For instructions, see Changing your privacy status.

Messages and Forums Notifications: A summary of all new and unread messages that exist in your sites. For more information, see Using Oncourse CL, how can I tell when someone adds a new message or forum posting in Message Center?.

Schedule overview: A small, summarized version of the Schedule tool. For more information, see Schedule summary.

Things to consider

  • Whatever you display inside Worksite Information will be viewable by everyone with access to your site.
  • Instructors are visible within a course site by default.
